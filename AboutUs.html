So... why are we called Vat19?

Good question. Maybe it's because Vat18 was already taken. Maybe it was just a random slur of words our founder blurted out after a rum-fueled bender. Whatever the case, evil boss Jamie Salvatori isn't telling.

What he will talk about is his passion for Vat19's winning formula. Since 2007, Vat19 has been collecting unique products from all over the world, choosing only the awesome stuff we'd be excited to receive ourselves, and finding a creative and entertaining way to present it to you.

Along the way, Jamie has collected a talented band of specialists that makes Vat19 work.
