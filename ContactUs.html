FAQs

  • When will my order ship?

    • All orders placed before 2PM CST are shipped the same business day. Otherwise, they are shipped the following business day. Please note that Saturday and Sunday are not considered business days.

  • How much is shipping?

    • We offer FREE SHIPPING worldwide.

  • Do you accept PayPal?

    • Yes! In addition to all major credit cards (Visa, MC, AMEX, and Discover), we also accept PayPal, ApplePay, and GooglePay.

  • Can you overnight my order?

    • Yes! We offer expedited delivery services via UPS. You can find out prices by using the shipping calculator on any product page or the shopping cart page.

  • Do you ship to APO/FPO addresses?

    • Yes! Please select the United States as your country during checkout.

  • Can I pay by check or money order?

    • No and yes. We do not accept personal checks, but we do accept money orders and cashier's checks.
